Monkey Mart

What is the Monkey Mart game?

Monkey Mart is a supermarket management game in which you take control of an adorable monkey who is in charge of managing and expanding the mart. Plant and harvest various food items to sell to your customers and earn money. Hire assistants to help with tasks such as planting, harvesting and selling, and upgrade your mart to attract even more customers.

How to Play

  • Arrow keys (or WASD) to Move
  • To plant, stand over empty plots of land
  • To harvest, stand over plants when they are ripe
  • To sell, place harvested food in the stands. Customers will pick them up and wait for you at the cash register
  • Hire employees, upgrade the equipment, and expand the supermarket to improve efficiency and attract more customers and sales
